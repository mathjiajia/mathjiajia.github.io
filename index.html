My name is Jia Jia (贾甲 in Chinese). I received my Ph.D. from National University of Singapore (NUS) under the supervision of Professor De-Qi Zhang in April 2023.

Before starting graduate study, I earned my BSc from Zhejiang University (ZJU) in June 2018.

My research interests are algebraic geometry, birational geometry.
