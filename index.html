My name is Jia Jia (贾甲 in Chinese). I am a postdoc at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC), Tsinghua University (THU) (mentor: Caucher Birkar).

I received my PhD from National University of Singapore (NUS) under the supervision of Professor De-Qi Zhang in April 2023.

Before that, I earned my BSc from Zhejiang University (ZJU) in June 2018.

My research interests are algebraic geometry, birational geometry.
